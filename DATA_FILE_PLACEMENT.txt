TripleDB fixed deployment filenames
===================================

Place JSON files here:

  data/tdb_core_interaction_index.json
  data/gene_alias_search_index_by_organism.json
  data/biogrid_source_index.json

Place CSV files here:

  downloads/tdb_core_interaction_table.csv
  downloads/gene_alias_table.csv
  downloads/biogrid_source_table.csv

Only the website deployment copies need these names. Your internal source and
intermediate files can retain your own provenance-oriented naming system.
